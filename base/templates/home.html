Racket club

The Calcutta Racket Club is a squash and racket club in Kolkata, India. It was founded in 1793, making it one of the oldest racket clubs in the world, and the first in the subcontinent of its seven squash courts, two are glass-backed international standard tournament courts. The club is the administrative headquarters of The West Bengal Squash Racket Association that is affiliated to the Squash Racket Federation of India. It has hosted the Kolkata International, an international squash tournament in 2005 and hosts all major squash activities and trainings in eastern India.
